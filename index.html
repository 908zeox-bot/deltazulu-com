About

A Playful Palate.
A Disciplined Hand.

Delta Zulu was founded by Dave Shull — a proven C-level executive with operating experience across enterprise technology, media, and consumer businesses. The firms he has led have generated billions in value.

Delta Zulu is not a fund. It is a personal holding company — capital deployed at conviction, not committee. Every investment comes with an operator at the table.

The thesis is simple: joyful ownership compounds. When you love what you own, you show up differently. You hold longer. You operate better. You attract the right partners.

Delta Zulu looks for assets where passion meets scarcity — technology before the crowd arrives, wine before the appellation is famous, airspace before the runway fills. The best investments feel like they belong to you long before the numbers agree.
