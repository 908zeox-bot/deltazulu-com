Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 11, 2026 — Delta Zulu LLC

1. Who We Are

Delta Zulu LLC ("Delta Zulu," "we," or "us") is a private holding company headquartered in Colorado, USA. This policy applies to internal software tools and services operated by Delta Zulu, including integrations with third-party platforms such as QuickBooks Online.

2. Information We Collect

Our internal tools access financial and operational data from connected services solely for internal reporting and analysis purposes. We do not collect personal information from third parties or the general public.

3. How We Use Information

Data accessed through third-party integrations (including QuickBooks Online) is used exclusively for:

  • Consolidated financial reporting across Delta Zulu entities
  • Internal operational analysis and decision-making
  • Record-keeping for Delta Zulu LLC and its subsidiaries

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data obtained through third-party integrations with any external parties. Access is limited to authorized personnel of Delta Zulu LLC.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect data, including encrypted storage of credentials and access tokens, and restricted network access via private infrastructure.

6. Data Retention

Financial data accessed through integrations is retained only as long as necessary for operational and legal compliance purposes.
